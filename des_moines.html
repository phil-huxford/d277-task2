Welcome to Des Moines!

Des Moines is the capital of Iowa and has a population of approximately 211,000 residence, as of 2022. Des Moines is an urban city located in the central Iowa. The median household income in Des Moines residence is $62,378, compaired to the state-wide median household income of $70,571. Des Moines was incorproated in 1851 as Fort Des Moines, which was shortened to Des Moines in 1857.

Des Moines derived its name from the French phrase "des Moines," which translates to "of the monks." This name was inspired by the Trappist monks who once lived along the banks of the Des Moines River.
