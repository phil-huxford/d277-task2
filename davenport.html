Welcome to Davenport!

Davenport was the first capital city of the state of Iowa. Davenport has a population of approximately 102,000 residence, as of 2022. Davenport is an urban city located in east-central Iowa. The median household income in Davenport residence is $59,890, compaired to the state-wide median household income of $70,571. Davenport was incorproated in 1836.

With its location on the Mississippi River, Davenport is often referred to as a "City of Bridges." It boasts an impressive collection of bridges, including the iconic Centennial Bridge, which connects Davenport with Illinois.
