Welcome to Iowa City!

Iowa City was the first capital city of the state of Iowa. Iowa City has a population of approximately 75,000 residence, as of 2022. Iowa City is an urban city located in east-central Iowa. The median household income in Iowa City residence is $54,879, compaired to the state-wide median household income of $70,571. Iowa City was incorproated in 1839.

In 1939, The University of Iowa established the nation's first blood bank to use preservative agents for long-term storage.
